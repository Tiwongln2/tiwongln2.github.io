About Me

When I reached high school, I took a Web Design course & knew immediately CS was the route for me. Through CS, I know I can impact others in the same way it impacted me.

  • What career aspirations/goals do you have for post-grad life? I'm definitely looking to work in industry full-time, and possibly going to grad school for research opportunities. In terms of CS work, as long as it involves coding, I'm open to opportunities :)

  • What do you enjoy outside CS? I'm an avid Marvel and The Walking Dead fan. You can always catch me watching the newest shows & reading new material for these universes. I love weightlifting, jump rope, and wrestling, as exercise is a big routine for me. Once the cold dissipates, I plan to refactor my garden. I'm also an aspiring investor.
